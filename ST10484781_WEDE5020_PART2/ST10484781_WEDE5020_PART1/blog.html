5 Quick Cleaning Hacks

Keeping your space tidy doesn't have to be time-consuming. Here are five quick cleaning hacks to make your life easier:

  • Lemon for Microwave: Place a bowl of water with lemon slices inside the microwave for 5 minutes, then wipe – stains and odors vanish easily.
  • Vinegar for Glass: Spray vinegar on windows and mirrors for a streak-free shine.
  • Baking Soda on Carpets: Sprinkle baking soda, leave for 10 minutes, then vacuum to remove odors.
  • Dryer Sheets for Dust: Use dryer sheets to wipe baseboards and surfaces – they repel dust.
  • Dish Soap for Grease: Mix warm water and dish soap to quickly cut through grease on kitchen counters.

Eco-Friendly Cleaning

Switching to eco-friendly cleaning products is not only better for the environment but also for your health. Here are some DIY eco-friendly cleaning solutions you can make at home:

  • All-Purpose Cleaner: Mix vinegar, water, and a few drops of essential oil for a natural spray.
  • Baking Soda Scrub: Use baking soda paste (with water) to scrub sinks, stovetops, and ovens.
  • Reusable Cloths: Replace paper towels with washable microfiber cloths.
  • Lemon Freshener: Rub half a lemon on cutting boards to sanitize and add freshness.
  • Open Windows: Instead of air fresheners, let fresh air circulate for natural ventilation.
